About LDS. Non-financial risk management.

Lonestar Defense was founded in 2019 as the brain-child and part of the family of companies better known as EDC Industries. We focus on assessing and managing non-financial risk to companies and individuals alike, by offering turn-key solutions that promote a culture of compliance and operational excellence to our customers.

Our services are what we do best.

As a security-focused company we deal in active tasks such as executive protection, resilience services, sensitive site exploitation (SSE) and critical infrastructure security, while also dealing in passive tasks such as inteligence collection, security consulting and training.
